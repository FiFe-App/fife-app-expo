# FiFe App Child Safety Standards Policy

**Last Updated:** June 2026

At FiFe App, the safety and well-being of our users are a top priority. We are committed to maintaining a safe and respectful environment and to preventing the use of our platform for child sexual abuse, exploitation, or any activity that harms minors.

This policy has been established in accordance with Google Play's Child Safety Standards and applicable child protection laws.

## 1. Zero Tolerance for Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

FiFe App maintains a strict zero-tolerance policy regarding child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

Any user account found to be involved in child sexual abuse material (CSAM), grooming, exploitation, trafficking, or any activity that endangers children will be permanently suspended and may be reported to relevant law enforcement authorities.

## 2. Prohibited Content and Conduct

The following activities are strictly prohibited on FiFe App:

* Sharing, uploading, distributing, requesting, or promoting child sexual abuse material (CSAM).
* Grooming, exploitation, manipulation, or solicitation of minors.
* Using the platform to facilitate illegal activities involving children.
* Any communication, content, or behavior that violates child protection laws.
* Impersonation of minors or misrepresentation of age for exploitative purposes.

Users who engage in these activities will be subject to immediate enforcement action.

## 3. Reporting Child Safety Concerns

Users may report content, profiles, or conversations that appear to violate this policy.

Reports can be submitted through the reporting features available within the application or by contacting us directly at:

**[info@fifeapp.hu](mailto:info@fifeapp.hu)**

When submitting a report, users should provide as much information as possible, including:

* The profile involved.
* Relevant messages or content.
* The reason for the report.
* Any additional information that may assist our investigation.

All reports related to child safety are reviewed with priority.

## 4. Handling Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM)

If FiFe App becomes aware of suspected CSAM or related violations, we may:

* Remove the content immediately.
* Suspend or permanently terminate the responsible account.
* Preserve evidence where legally required.
* Report the matter to relevant authorities, including child protection organizations and law enforcement agencies.
* Cooperate with investigations as required by law.

## 5. Compliance with Child Protection Laws

FiFe App complies with applicable child safety and child protection laws, including laws related to:

* Identification and reporting of child sexual exploitation.
* Protection of minors online.
* Privacy and security of user data.
* Cooperation with lawful requests from regulatory and law enforcement authorities.

## 6. Child Safety Contact

FiFe App has designated a contact point for child safety matters.

For reports, inquiries, or concerns regarding child safety on the platform, please contact:

**Email:** [info@fifeapp.hu](mailto:info@fifeapp.hu)

This contact point is responsible for reviewing child safety reports and coordinating appropriate action when necessary.

## 7. Safety Monitoring and Prevention

FiFe App reserves the right to investigate reports of harmful, abusive, illegal, or exploitative behavior.

We continually review and improve our moderation, reporting, and safety processes to help identify and prevent misuse of the platform.

## 8. Enforcement

Violations of this policy may result in:

* Content removal.
* Account restrictions.
* Temporary suspension.
* Permanent account termination.
* Reporting to law enforcement or child protection authorities when required.

FiFe App reserves the right to update this policy to reflect changes in applicable laws, regulations, or platform requirements.

## 9. Immediate Danger

If you believe a child is in immediate danger, contact your local emergency services or law enforcement authority immediately.

If you encounter content or behavior on FiFe App that may violate this policy, please report it through the application or email us at:

**[info@fifeapp.hu](mailto:info@fifeapp.hu)**
